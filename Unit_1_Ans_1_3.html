Unit 1 - Answer 1.3) Main features of threats to individuals: -

Victims of cyber crimes are impacted in various manners, depending on their losses and how they perceive the world will view them. Regardless of age, victims begin to suffer, stress & depression, from happy to nervous wreck, relationships are impacted and when everything gets too much where they cannot cope with the losses then the worst case outcomes are known to be suicide. Especially, in younger students but also mature individuals that cannot cope with losing their life savings to cyber criminals.

For example : -

  1. Cyber Bullying : -

    2. School children are impacted psychologically. they become withdrawn and anti-social. They can turn from “happy go lucky individuals to a nervous wreck”. Some are known to have committed suicide.

  2. Financial Crime : -

    3. People that lose their life savings suffer mental problems and they too resort to suicide. Others become very suspicious of everyone and are afraid to interact socially. Their relationship may suffer too.

  3. Ransom Demands: -

    4. Some people can pay up and forget it. Others do not cope well.They suffer psychological damage and spend their life trying to pay or to find the perpetrator. End up wasting their lives trying to find a niddle in a haystack. Feeling of revenge and what they intend to do, once the cyber criminal is caught.
