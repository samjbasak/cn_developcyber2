Unit 1 - Answer 2.5) A review of the hierarchy of damage and where major threats sit on it.

For example : -

  1. DISTRIBUTED DENIAL OF SERVICE.
    2. Companies suffer from loss of revenue due to service outage, bad publicity and loss of customer faith and where the customer simply opt to take their business elsewhere as happened with the TalkTalk hacking. Customer do not feel safe but violated and invasion of their privacy.

  2. CROSS PLATFORM MALWARE.
    3. Personal details stolen can be used against people to blackmail them so they suffer from anxiety and depression of what can happen to them, who knows what and how much is a constant fear. People lose trusting computer systems or giving out their personal data even when it is needed to get some services. Some people are extremely weary of opening any form of email attachments or lose the faith to download any application for fear of being a virus infecting their computers. Generally, people suffer stress, depression and anxiety. They lose faith in computer systems and some even stop using internet banking and limit their interactions with only close and trusted family members.
